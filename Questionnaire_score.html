QUESTIONNAIRE BASED ON ISAA

Grading Instructions

Rarely (up to 20%)

Behavior occurs up to 20% of the time and is considered normal for age and socio-educational background.

Score: 1

Sometimes (21-40%)

Behavior occurs 21-40% of the time. Some behaviors may raise concern but are generally within normal limits for age and socio-educational background.

Score: 2

Frequently (41-60%)

Behavior occurs 41-60% of the time, regularly interfering with daily functioning, and is considered definitely disabling.

Score: 3

Mostly (61-80%)

Behavior occurs 61-80% of the time, often without a clear trigger, significantly hindering daily activities. The person requires assistance with daily tasks.

Score: 4

Always (81-100%)

Behavior occurs nearly all the time, is rarely appropriate, and is considered a major handicap. The person is completely dependent for daily activities.

Score: 5

* The minimum score that can be obtained is 40.

* The maximum score that can be obtained is 200.
